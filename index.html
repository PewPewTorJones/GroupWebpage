Early Fortnite Lore

Chapter 1: Start


Season 1-2 - The Island and The Visitor Arrival:

  • Story was minimal at first
  • Objects like the Durr Burger, meteor fragments, and other anomonalous
  • Early hints of the "loop"
  • The first major piece of lore revealed was about the approaching meteor

Season 3-4 - The Meteor and The Visitor:

  • Giant meteor was placed on the map (players could land there and loot chests
  • During Season 4 the meteor crashed into Dusty Depot (renamed to Dusty Divot and the POI was changed)
  • Was revealed that inside the Meteor was a figure named "The Visitor" who was a member of the team named the Seven
  • He built a rocket which was later laucnhed and created a reality tear known as the Zero Point rift

Season 5 - Rifts and Reality Breaks:

  • The rocket’s tear opened rifts that pulled in objects from other dimensions.
  • New areas appeared: Viking Village and the Paradise Palms desert biome.
  • The Zero Point began to destabilize.

Season 6: The Cube ("Kevin")

  • A rift lightning strike created the Purple Cube (Kevin).
  • Kevin traveled the map, leaving runes and corrupt zones.
  • The cube dissolved into Loot Lake, turning the water bouncy.
  • Loot Lake rose into the air, forming the Floating Island.

Season 7: The Ice King Arrives

  • An iceberg collided with the island, introducing the Ice King.
  • A snowy biome and castle appeared.
  • The Ice King covered the island in snow.
  • The Prisoner (Fire King) escaped captivity.

Season 8: Fire vs. Ice

  • The Prisoner unleashed volcanic activity on the island.
  • The Volcano erupted, destroying Tilted Towers and Retail Row.
  • The Zero Point’s influence increased across the map.

Season 9: Neo Tilted & the Monster

  • A giant creature (the Devourer) awakened beneath Polar Peak.
  • The island built the Mecha Team Leader robot to combat it.
  • The Mecha defeated the monster but exposed the Zero Point at Loot Lake.
  • The Zero Point became unstable.

Season 10 (X): Reality Collapsing

  • The unstable Zero Point caused massive reality distortions.
  • Old locations returned as altered rift zones.
  • The Visitor and additional members of The Seven attempted to stabilize reality.
  • The Zero Point exploded, collapsing the island into a black hole.

The End of Chapter 1

  • The Black Hole event erased the island.
  • Players were left in darkness for two days.
  • Chapter 2 eventually began with a new island.
