Early Fortnite Lore


By: Nate V. and Tor J.

Fortnite was released in 2017, and at first nobody expected it to become one of the biggest and most impactful stories in gaming. Chapter 1 introduced the island, the mysterious Zero Point, and the forces that would shape the future of the game. From meteors to massive live events, Fortnite set the foundation for becoming one of the most popular games in the world. Below is a summary of the key events that defined this chapter.

Chapter 1: Start

Season 1-2 - The Island and The Visitor Arrival:

  1. Story was minimal at first
  2. Objects like the Durr Burger, meteor fragments, and other anomalous items appeared
  3. Early hints of the "loop"
  4. The first major piece of lore revealed was about the approaching meteor

Season 3-4 - The Meteor and The Visitor:

  1. A giant meteor was placed on the map (players could land there and loot chests)
  2. During Season 4 the meteor crashed into Dusty Depot (renamed Dusty Divot)
  3. Inside the meteor was "The Visitor," a member of The Seven
  4. He built a rocket which created the Zero Point rift

Season 5 - Rifts and Reality Breaks:

  1. The rocket’s tear opened rifts bringing objects from other dimensions
  2. New POIs: Viking Village and Paradise Palms
  3. The Zero Point began destabilizing

Season 6 - The Cube ("Kevin")

  1. A lightning strike formed Kevin the Cube
  2. Kevin traveled the map leaving runes
  3. The cube melted into Loot Lake turning it bouncy
  4. The lake rose into the sky becoming the Floating Island

Season 7 - The Ice King Arrives

  1. An iceberg collided with the island
  2. A snow castle POI appeared
  3. The Ice King blanketed the island in snow
  4. The Prisoner escaped captivity

Season 8 - Fire vs. Ice

  1. The Prisoner unleashed volcanic activity
  2. The Volcano destroyed Tilted Towers & Retail Row
  3. The Zero Point grew stronger

Season 9 - Neo Tilted & the Monster

  1. A giant creature awakened under Polar Peak
  2. The Mecha Team Leader was built to stop it
  3. The robot won but exposed the Zero Point
  4. The Zero Point became unstable

Season 10 (X) - Reality Collapsing

  1. The Zero Point distorted reality
  2. Old locations returned altered
  3. The Seven attempted to stabilize reality
  4. The Zero Point exploded, creating the black hole

Chapter 2: A New Island

Season 1 – The Island Reborn

  1. The new island emerges after the Black Hole
  2. The Zero Point is hidden underwater
  3. New POIs and secrets
  4. Hints are made to future events

Season 2 – GHOST vs SHADOW

  1. Two spy factions battle for control
  2. Midas (Ghost Leader) builds the Doomsday Device
  3. The Device event floods the island

Season 3 – Flooded Map

  1. The island becomes mostly water
  2. The Zero Point starts reacting again

Season 4 – Nexus War

  1. Marvel heroes arrive due to the unstable Zero Point
  2. Galactus absorbs the Zero Point but is defeated

Season 5 – Hunters and Portals

  1. The exposed Zero Point cracks open
  2. Agent Jones recruits characters from other realities

Season 6 – Primal

  1. The Foundation seals the Zero Point in a spire
  2. The island becomes wild and primal

Season 7 – Alien Invasion

  1. Aliens abduct POIs
  2. Dr. Slone leads an IO mission to stop them

Season 8 – The Cube Queen

  1. Cubes spread corruption across the map
  2. The Cube Queen attempts to destroy reality
  3. The Foundation returns and saves Jonesy

End of Chapter 2

  1. The Cube Queen flips the entire island
  2. This reveals the Chapter 3 island underneath
